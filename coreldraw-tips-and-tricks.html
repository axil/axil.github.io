CorelDraw Tips and Tricks

  • Copying objects
    • num-plus duplicate with 0 distance [*]
    • ctrl-d duplicate with distance set in the toolbar [†]
    • right-click when dragging
    • space when dragging
    • ctrl-r repeat last action
[*]Non-remappable to other keys other than by creating a macros
[†]Starts 'duplication mode':  records copied movement and rotation and applies them (instead of the distance from the toolbar and zero rotation) for subsequent ctrl-d strokes until another object is selected (resetting distance and rotation to original).
